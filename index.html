History of Pottery

Pottery is one of the oldest human inventions, dating back to over 20,000 years. Across civilizations—from ancient China to the Mediterranean—the art of shaping clay has served practical, artistic, and cultural purposes. Today, pottery remains a timeless craft that combines creativity, skill, and tradition.

Pottery Classes

  • Hand-Building Basics: Master coils, slabs, and pinching techniques.
  • Glazing & Finishing: Explore colors, textures, and firing methods.
